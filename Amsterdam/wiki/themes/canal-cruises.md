# Canal Cruises

## Overview

A canal cruise is one of the quintessential Amsterdam experiences. The city's [canal ring](../places/canal-ring.md) -- 165 canals, 100+ km of waterways, and 1,281 bridges -- is best appreciated from the water. **[Multi-source]**

## Options

### Hop-on Hop-off Boat

CitySightseeing operates combined bus + boat hop-on hop-off service with free audioguide in 16 languages, free wifi, maps, and mobile app. **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

| Ticket Type | 1 Day | 2 Days |
|---|---|---|
| Bus + boat combo | EUR 37.50 | EUR 47.50 |
| Boat only | EUR 29.50 | EUR 37.00 |
| Bus only | EUR 29.50 | EUR 37.00 |

Children under 4 free; ages 4--13 half price. **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

### Standard Canal Cruise

Various operators run 1-hour cruises through the main canals. Typically EUR 15--20 per person. Available throughout the day from multiple departure points near [Centraal Station](../practical/getting-around.md) and the [Rijksmuseum](../places/rijksmuseum.md). **[Unverified -- pricing not specified in sources]**

### Evening Cruise

Dinner cruises and illuminated evening tours offer a different perspective on the canal houses. **[Unverified]**

## Tips

- The [I Amsterdam Card](../practical/budget-tips.md) includes a canal cruise **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**
- Whisper boats (electric, self-drive) are available for a more intimate experience -- also at [Keukenhof](../places/keukenhof.md) **[Source: keukenhof]**
