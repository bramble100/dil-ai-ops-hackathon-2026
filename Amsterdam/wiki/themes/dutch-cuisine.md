# Dutch Cuisine

## Overview

Dutch cuisine is hearty and practical, shaped by the country's maritime and agricultural traditions. Amsterdam's dining scene ranges from traditional Dutch fare to global cuisines reflecting the city's multicultural character. **[Multi-source]**

## Must-Try Dutch Foods

| Dish | Description | Where |
|---|---|---|
| **Stroopwafels** | Two thin waffles sandwiching warm caramel syrup | Street markets, [Albert Cuyp Market](../places/albert-cuyp-market.md) |
| **Haring (herring)** | Raw herring with onions and pickles | Street stalls, fish shops |
| **Bitterballen** | Deep-fried crispy balls of meat ragout, served with mustard | Bars and cafes (the quintessential Dutch bar snack) |
| **Poffertjes** | Mini fluffy pancakes with butter and powdered sugar | Street vendors, pancake houses |
| **Stamppot** | Mashed potatoes mixed with vegetables (kale, sauerkraut, etc.) | Traditional restaurants |
| **Pannenkoeken** | Dutch pancakes (larger, thinner than American) with sweet or savory toppings | Pancake houses |
| **Kroket** | Deep-fried roll filled with meat ragout | Snack bars, vending machines (FEBO) |

**[Source: cosas-tipicas]**

## Cheese

The Netherlands is one of the world's great cheese nations. Key varieties:

- **Gouda** -- ranges from young and mild (jong) to hard and intense (oud/extra belegen)
- **Edam** -- distinctive red wax coating, mild and slightly nutty
- **Leiden** -- with cumin seeds

Visit a cheese farm at [Zaanse Schans](../places/zaanse-schans.md) or see the traditional cheese market in [Volendam/Edam](../places/volendam-marken.md) (Wednesdays, July--August). **[Source: viajeros-callejeros]**

## Drinks

- **Beer** -- Heineken was born in Amsterdam; visit the Heineken Experience. Craft beer scene is also growing.
- **Jenever** -- traditional Dutch juniper-flavored spirit, ancestor of gin. Try at a proeflokaal (tasting house) like De Drie Fleschjes near [Dam Square](../places/dam-square.md). **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
- **Coffee** -- the Dutch have a strong coffee culture; "koffie" is a social ritual

## Dining at Die Port van Cleve

The historic [Die Port van Cleve](../hotels/die-port-van-cleve.md) hotel features:
- **Hulscher's Restaurant** -- famous for steaks
- **Bouillon d'Amsterdam** -- French-Dutch cuisine
- **Bar-Bodega De Blauwe Parade** -- drinks and bites in a historic setting
