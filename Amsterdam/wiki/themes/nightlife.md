# Amsterdam Nightlife

## Overview

Amsterdam's nightlife reflects its famously tolerant culture. From historic brown cafes (bruine kroegen) to the [Red Light District](../places/red-light-district.md), the city offers a wide range of evening entertainment. **[Multi-source]**

## Key Areas

- **Leidseplein** -- Amsterdam's entertainment square; clubs, theaters, bars **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
- **Rembrandtplein** -- another major nightlife hub with bars and clubs **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
- **Red Light District (De Wallen)** -- the famous window district; also home to [Oude Kerk](../places/red-light-district.md) **[Multi-source]**
- **Jordaan** -- cozy neighborhood with intimate bars and brown cafes **[Unverified]**
- **De Pijp** -- trendy neighborhood with the [Albert Cuyp Market](../places/albert-cuyp-market.md) area having many bars and restaurants **[Unverified]**

## Brown Cafes (Bruine Kroegen)

Traditional Dutch pubs with dark wood interiors stained by centuries of tobacco smoke. Great for beer, jenever (Dutch gin), and bitterballen. **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**

De Drie Fleschjes, a 17th-century tasting house behind the [Nieuwe Kerk](../places/dam-square.md), is one of the most atmospheric. **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**

## Coffee Shops

Not to be confused with cafes (which serve coffee), "coffee shops" in Amsterdam sell cannabis products. The Dutch policy of regulated tolerance means these establishments operate openly but under strict rules. **[Source: top10-amszterdam]**

## Night Transport

Night buses run from 0:30 to 7:00 AM, numbered 281-293, all passing through Centraal Station. Night bus ticket: EUR 5.70 (valid 1.5 hours with unlimited transfers). 24/48/72/96-hour transport passes are also valid on night buses. **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

## Museum Evenings

The [Van Gogh Museum](../places/van-gogh-museum.md) hosts cocktail evenings with DJ on Friday nights during peak season -- a unique cultural nightlife experience. **[Source: top10-amszterdam]**
