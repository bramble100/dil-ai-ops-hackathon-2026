# Budget Tips for Amsterdam

## Tourist Cards

### I Amsterdam Card

The all-in-one city pass covering transport + 90 attractions: **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

| Duration | Price | Break-even (approx.) |
|---|---|---|
| 1 day | EUR 67 | ~2 major museums + transport |
| 2 days | EUR 94 | ~3 museums + transport |
| 3 days | EUR 115 | ~4 museums + transport |
| 4 days | EUR 130 | ~5 museums + transport |

**Includes**: GVB transport, canal cruise, museum entries. **Does NOT include**: Anne Frank House.

### Holland Museum Card (Museumkaart)

Grants free entry to 400+ museums nationwide. Worth it if visiting 3+ museums across the Netherlands. **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

### GVB Transport Passes

If you mainly need transport (not museum entries): **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

- 24h: EUR 10 / 48h: EUR 16 / 72h: EUR 21.50 / 96h: EUR 27.50

## Free & Low-Cost Activities

- Walk the [canal ring](../places/canal-ring.md) -- Amsterdam's greatest attraction is free **[Multi-source]**
- [Vondelpark](../places/vondelpark.md) -- free entry, free summer theater **[Source: top10-amszterdam]**
- [Begijnhof](../places/begijnhof.md) -- free entry to the medieval courtyard **[Source: top10-amszterdam]**
- [NEMO](../places/nemo-science-museum.md) rooftop terrace -- free city panorama, no ticket needed **[Source: top10-amszterdam]**
- Ferry across IJ river -- free, great views **[Unverified]**
- Diamond workshop viewing -- free near Rijksmuseum **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**
- [Albert Cuyp Market](../places/albert-cuyp-market.md) -- free to browse, affordable street food **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

## Transport Savings

- **FlixBus** for day trips to [Rotterdam](../places/rotterdam.md) -- cheaper than train **[Source: viajeros-callejeros]**
- **Walk** -- central Amsterdam is very compact
- **Cycle** -- rental bikes are cheaper than daily transport passes for active travelers
- **Night buses** -- EUR 5.70 per ride; covered by 24h+ passes **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

## Accommodation

- Stay outside the canal ring for cheaper rates in the same category **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
- Book peak season (April--August) well in advance
- Look for hotel promotions: [Die Port van Cleve](../hotels/die-port-van-cleve.md) offers Spring sale (code "Spring30" for 30% off) **[Source: port-van-cleve]**

## Food Savings

- Eat at markets: [Albert Cuyp Market](../places/albert-cuyp-market.md) and street food stalls
- Stroopwafels and herring from street vendors -- authentic and cheap
- Supermarkets (Albert Heijn, Jumbo) for picnic supplies
- Skip tourist-trap restaurants directly on [Dam Square](../places/dam-square.md)
