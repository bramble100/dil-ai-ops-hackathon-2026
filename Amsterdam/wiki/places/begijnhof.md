# Begijnhof

The Begijnhof is a medieval enclosed courtyard (*hofje*) hidden in the heart of Amsterdam's city center, just a short walk from [Dam Square](dam-square.md) and the [Bloemenmarkt](bloemenmarkt.md). Stepping through its unassuming entrance from the busy Spui square, visitors find themselves in one of the most peaceful and atmospheric spots in the city. [Multi-source]

## History

The courtyard was originally established as a sanctuary for **Beguines** (*begijnen*) -- women who chose to live a religious life in community, similar to nuns, but **without taking permanent vows**. Unlike nuns in a convent, Beguines retained the freedom to leave the community and marry if they chose. This semi-religious order was common in the Low Countries from the medieval period onward. [Source: top10-amszterdam]

## Highlights

### Holland's Oldest Wooden House

The Begijnhof contains **Holland's oldest surviving wooden house**, dating from approximately **1420**. After a series of devastating fires in Amsterdam, wooden construction was banned in the city, making this house a rare survivor from the medieval period. [Source: top10-amszterdam]

### Architecture and Atmosphere

The courtyard is lined with small, well-maintained houses surrounding a central garden with a church. The architecture spans several centuries, creating a layered sense of Amsterdam's history in a single enclosed space. The quiet atmosphere stands in stark contrast to the busy commercial streets just meters away. [Multi-source]

## Today

The Begijnhof remains in use as **private residences for single women**, continuing a tradition of female community living that stretches back to the medieval period. Visitors are welcome to enter and look around, but should be respectful of the fact that people live here. [Source: top10-amszterdam]

## Tips

- The entrance is easy to miss -- look for a small doorway on Spui square. [Unverified]
- Keep noise to a minimum and respect the residential nature of the courtyard. [Multi-source]
- Combine with a visit to the nearby [Bloemenmarkt](bloemenmarkt.md) on the Singel canal. [Unverified]
