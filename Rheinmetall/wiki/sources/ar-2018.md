# Annual Report 2018 (Geschaeftsbericht 2018)

**File:** `raw/DE0007030009-JA-2018-EQ-D-00.pdf` (230 pages, German)

## Key Facts

- Revenue: 6,148 MioEUR (+4.3%)
- Operating Result: 491 MioEUR; Margin: 8.0%
- Defence order intake: EUR 5.5 billion (record, including Land 400 Phase 2)
- Defence backlog: EUR 8,577 MioEUR
- Automotive margin: 8.9% (record)
- Investment grade rating restored (April 2018)
- Tragic Somerset West incident (8 fatalities)

## Significance

Management declared the beginning of the "defence super cycle." Record automotive margin (8.9%) masked growing structural headwinds (diesel crisis, WLTP). Historical data tables extend back to 2012, providing the longest time series in any single report.
