# Earnings Call Transcript - FY2020 Results

**File:** `raw/2021-03-18_Rheinmetall_Transcript_Conference_Call_FY2020.pdf` (32 pages, English)
**Date:** 18 March 2021, 14:00-15:15 CET
**Speakers:** Armin Papperger (CEO), Helmut Merch (CFO)

## Key Takeaways

- "Solid set of financials under these circumstances" -- op. result 446m, FCF 217m despite COVID
- **Pivotal strategic statement:** "The goal is to create stronger emphasis to Defence and to reduce our dependency from internal combustion engines"
- ONE Rheinmetall restructuring: 5 divisions, pistons non-core, Goldman Sachs advising on sale
- Defence margins broke double digits (11.1%) for the first time; W&A Q4 margin ~30%
- Defence backlog: EUR 12.9bn + EUR 3.6bn shadow backlog
- New 155mm ammunition: 70+ km range (vs ~40km industry standard)
- Counter-drone/swarm systems development (lessons from Azerbaijan-Armenia conflict)
- M&A pipeline: "30 to 50 different companies" under review, with EUR 1bn+ cash available
- ESG targets introduced: 20% weight in long-term compensation; UN Global Compact application
- Minimum 10% EBIT hurdle for all divisions
- Pistons sale: ~100 interested parties, shortlisted to 20-30, target signing Q3 2021
- Service business to double to ~EUR 1bn by 2025

## Notable Quotes

> "This difference in the business development accelerated our approach to initiate the transformation." -- Papperger (the automotive-to-defense pivot statement)

> "As long as Germany is in the NATO, it doesn't matter which coalition is really ruling this country." -- Papperger on election risk

> "Nothing is wrong. But you know that we are really conservative. We want to deliver 100%." -- Papperger on conservative guidance
