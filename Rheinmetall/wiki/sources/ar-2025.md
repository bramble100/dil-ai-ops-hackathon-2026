# Annual Report 2025 (Geschaeftsbericht 2025)

**File:** `raw/RheinmetallAGAnnualReport2025.pdf` (286 pages, German)

## Key Facts

- **Reporting period:** FY2025 (1 Jan - 31 Dec 2025)
- **Language:** German
- **Auditor:** Deloitte GmbH, Munich/Dusseldorf (unqualified opinion)
- **Structure:** Based on IFRS Accounting Standards; first report with Power Systems as discontinued operations

## Headline Numbers

| Metric | 2025 | 2024 |
|--------|------|------|
| Revenue | 9,935 MioEUR | 7,715 MioEUR |
| Operating Result | 1,841 MioEUR | 1,389 MioEUR |
| Operating Margin | 18.5% | 18.0% |
| EBIT | 1,684 MioEUR | 1,270 MioEUR |
| ROCE | 33.5% | 25.7% |
| FCF (continuing) | 1,218 MioEUR | 1,056 MioEUR |
| Backlog | 63,800 MioEUR | 46,943 MioEUR |

## Key Strategic Content

- **Power Systems discontinued:** Civilian activities classified under IFRS 5 from 17 December 2025
- **Board restructuring:** CEO Papperger renewed for 5 years; new CFO (Neumann) and COO (Gansauge)
- **Sigmar Gabriel** appointed to supervisory board (May 2025)
- **2030 targets:** Revenue ~EUR 50bn, margin >20%, CCR >50%
- **Ukraine factory:** New production facilities built in coordination with Ukrainian government
- **Unterluess:** Largest European ammunition factory opened (September 2025)
- **LRMV JV with Leonardo:** Italian MBT/IFV partnership
- **MGCS Project Company:** Founded with KNDS and Thales
- **F-35 production:** Fuselage mid-section at Weeze
- **NVL acquisition:** Naval Vessels Lurssen completed February 2026
- **EURO STOXX 50:** Index membership achieved
