# Annual Report 2019 (Geschaeftsbericht 2019)

**File:** `raw/DE0007030009-JA-2019-EQ-D-01.pdf` (248 pages, German)

## Key Facts

- Revenue: 6,255 MioEUR (peak pre-COVID group revenue)
- Operating Result: 505 MioEUR; Margin: 8.1%
- Defence revenue: 3,519 MioEUR (margin 9.1%)
- Automotive revenue: 2,736 MioEUR (margin 6.7% -- declining)
- Defence backlog crossed EUR 10 billion for the first time
- Export restrictions cost ~EUR 90 million (Saudi Arabia, South Africa)

## Significance

Peak pre-COVID revenue year. Defence margins reached 9.1%, validating the "super cycle" thesis. However, automotive was already in structural decline (-6.8% revenue). The two segments' diverging trajectories foreshadowed the eventual split.
