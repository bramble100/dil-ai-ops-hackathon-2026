## Place Pages - Amsterdam

- [Rijksmuseum](places/rijksmuseum.md) - Netherlands' largest museum: Night Watch, Milkmaid, 800 years of art
- [Van Gogh Museum](places/van-gogh-museum.md) - World's largest Van Gogh collection: 200+ paintings, 500 drawings
- [Anne Frank House](places/anne-frank-house.md) - WWII hiding place museum; book online far in advance
- [Dam Square](places/dam-square.md) - Historic heart: Royal Palace, Nieuwe Kerk, National Monument
- [Canal Ring](places/canal-ring.md) - UNESCO World Heritage: 165 canals, 1,281 bridges, houseboats
- [Vondelpark](places/vondelpark.md) - Amsterdam's largest park: free theater, playgrounds, King's Day market
- [NEMO Science Museum](places/nemo-science-museum.md) - Ship-shaped 5-floor interactive science center; free rooftop
- [Red Light District](places/red-light-district.md) - De Wallen: Dutch tolerance policy, Oude Kerk, coffee shops
- [Albert Cuyp Market](places/albert-cuyp-market.md) - Amsterdam's most famous street market in De Pijp
- [Begijnhof](places/begijnhof.md) - Medieval courtyard with Holland's oldest wooden house (1420)
- [Bloemenmarkt](places/bloemenmarkt.md) - World's only floating flower market on the Singel canal

- [Keukenhof](places/keukenhof.md) - World's largest flower garden; spring only (2026: Mar 19 - May 10)

- [Delft](places/delft.md) - Blue ceramics, fairy-tale canals, Vermeer Centre

- [Kinderdijk](places/kinderdijk.md) - 19 UNESCO World Heritage windmills

- [Volendam & Marken](places/volendam-marken.md) - Traditional fishing villages, Edam cheese market
- [Giethoorn](places/giethoorn.md) - "Venice of the Netherlands" car-free canal village
- [The Hague](places/hague.md) - Government seat, Mauritshuis, Scheveningen beach
- [Maastricht](places/maastricht.md) - Burgundian culture, Netherlands' oldest city
- [Cruquius Museum](places/cruquius-museum.md) - World's largest steam engine; Dutch water engineering history
