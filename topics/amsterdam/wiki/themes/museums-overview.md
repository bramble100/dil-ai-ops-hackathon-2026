# Amsterdam Museums

## Overview

Amsterdam is one of Europe's great museum cities, with world-class collections spanning art, history, science, and maritime heritage. Most major museums are concentrated around Museumplein (Museum Square) in the south of the city center. **[Multi-source]**

## Booking Tips

- **Online tickets required** for Van Gogh, Rijksmuseum, Anne Frank House -- walk-up not possible **[Multi-source]**
- **Anne Frank House sells out weeks ahead** -- book as early as possible **[Multi-source]**
- **Holland Museum Card** (Museumkaart) gives free entry to 400+ museums nationwide -- worth it if visiting 3+ museums **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**
- **I Amsterdam Card** bundles transport + 90 attractions -- see [Budget Tips](../practical/budget-tips.md) **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

## Beyond the Big Three

Amsterdam has dozens more museums worth exploring:

- **Moco Museum** -- Banksy, Warhol, and modern art at Museumplein **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
- **Verzetsmuseum** (Resistance Museum) -- Dutch resistance during WWII **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
- **Wereldmuseum** (formerly Tropenmuseum) -- world cultures **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
- **Body Worlds** -- anatomical exhibition **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
- **Amsterdam Museum** (Amsterdams Historisch Museum) -- city history **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
- **Madame Tussauds** -- wax figures on [Dam Square](../places/dam-square.md) **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**
- **Heineken Experience** -- brewery tour and tasting **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**
- **Diamond workshops** -- near Rijksmuseum, free to watch artisans at work **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

## Outside Amsterdam

- [Cruquius Museum](../places/cruquius-museum.md) -- world's largest steam engine, 20 km from Amsterdam **[Source: cruquius-wikipedia]**
- [Keukenhof](../places/keukenhof.md) -- not a museum per se, but an essential spring visit **[Source: keukenhof]**
