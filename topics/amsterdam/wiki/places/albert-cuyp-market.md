# Albert Cuyp Market

The Albert Cuyp Market (*Albert Cuypmarkt*) is Amsterdam's most famous street market and one of the largest daily markets in the Netherlands. Located in the lively **De Pijp** neighborhood, it offers a vibrant, authentic slice of local Amsterdam life far removed from the tourist-heavy city center. [Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]

## What You'll Find

The market stretches along Albert Cuypstraat and features a wide range of stalls selling:

- **Fresh food** -- fruit, vegetables, fish, meat, baked goods
- **Cheese** -- Dutch varieties including Gouda and Edam
- **Flowers** -- fresh-cut bouquets and plants
- **Clothing** -- both new and vintage
- **Household items** -- kitchenware, fabrics, accessories

[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]

## Why Visit

The Albert Cuyp Market is an excellent destination for **budget shopping** and for experiencing how Amsterdammers actually shop and eat. The food stalls are particularly good for trying Dutch street food at reasonable prices. The surrounding De Pijp neighborhood is one of Amsterdam's most multicultural and up-and-coming areas, with plenty of cafes, restaurants, and independent shops to explore after the market. [Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]
