# Keukenhof

The world's most famous spring flower garden, located near the town of Lisse, approximately 35 km southwest of Amsterdam. [Multi-source]

## Overview

Keukenhof is a seasonal garden open only during spring, showcasing millions of blooming flowers across its grounds. It is the premier destination for anyone wanting to experience the Dutch tulip tradition at its most spectacular. [Multi-source]

### 2026 Season

- **Open:** March 19 - May 10, 2026 [Source: keukenhof]
- **Tickets:** Online only via [keukenhof.nl](https://keukenhof.nl) [Source: keukenhof]

## Best Time to Visit

The ideal timing depends on which flowers you want to see: [Source: viajeros-callejeros]

- **Early season (late March - early April):** Crocuses, daffodils, hyacinths, and early tulips
- **Later season (mid-April - May):** Larger tulips in full bloom

### Avoiding Crowds

- Visit before 10:30 AM or after 4:00 PM for a quieter experience [Source: viajeros-callejeros]
- Monday through Wednesday tend to be the quietest days of the week [Source: viajeros-callejeros]
