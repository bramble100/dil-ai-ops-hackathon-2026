# Bloemenmarkt (Floating Flower Market)

The Bloemenmarkt is the **world's only floating flower market**. Its stalls are set up on houseboats moored along the Singel canal, one of the main canals of the [Canal Ring](canal-ring.md), in central Amsterdam near [Dam Square](dam-square.md) and the [Begijnhof](begijnhof.md). [Multi-source]

## What You'll Find

The market sells a colorful array of:

- **Fresh flowers** -- seasonal bouquets, roses, sunflowers, and more
- **Tulip bulbs** -- the iconic Dutch souvenir, available in countless varieties
- **Plants and seeds** -- both common and exotic
- **Souvenirs** -- Dutch-themed gifts, clogs, and flower-related items

[Multi-source]

## The Experience

Even for those not planning to buy, the Bloemenmarkt is worth visiting for the **scent alone** -- the combined fragrance of thousands of fresh flowers wafting over the canal water is one of Amsterdam's most distinctive sensory experiences. The market has been operating in this location since 1862, and while it has become increasingly tourist-oriented over the decades, it remains a beloved Amsterdam institution. [Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]

## Tips

- If buying tulip bulbs to take home, check customs regulations for your destination country before purchasing. [Unverified]
- The market is at its most photogenic in spring when tulip season is in full swing. [Unverified]
- Combine a visit with a stroll along the [Canal Ring](canal-ring.md) or a stop at the nearby [Begijnhof](begijnhof.md). [Multi-source]
