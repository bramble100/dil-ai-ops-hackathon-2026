# Die Port van Cleve

## Overview

A historic hotel in the heart of Amsterdam, located directly behind the Nieuwe Kerk on [Dam Square](../places/dam-square.md) and next to Magna Plaza shopping center. Many of Amsterdam's top attractions are within walking distance. **[Source: port-van-cleve]**

## History

> "From our humble beginnings as a brewery in 1864 to becoming one of Amsterdam's most beloved hotels, Die Port van Cleve has been a witness to history, culture, and innovation."

The story begins with Gerard Heineken meeting the Hulscher brothers, who proposed opening a beer house inside the brewery. With Heineken's approval, Die Port van Cleve was born over 155 years ago. The hotel is currently celebrating its **155th anniversary** with year-long promotions. **[Source: port-van-cleve]**
