# Keukenhof Official Website

**Source:** Keukenhof.nl (official website)
**Language:** English

## Summary

Official website content for Keukenhof, the world-famous tulip garden in Lisse, Netherlands. Covers the 2026 season dates, ticketing, visiting tips, events schedule, activities, and visitor services.

## 2026 Season

- **Opening:** March 19, 2026
- **Closing:** May 10, 2026
- **Tickets:** Available online (advance purchase recommended)

## Best Time to Visit

### By Flowers
- **Early season (late March - early April):** Crocuses, daffodils, hyacinths
- **Later season (mid-April - May):** Larger tulips in full bloom

### By Crowds
- **Quieter times:** Before 10:30 AM or after 4:00 PM
- **Quietest days:** Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday

## Events Schedule (2026)

| Event | Dates |
|-------|-------|
| Alstroemeria & Calla Show | April 16-21 |
| Flower Parade (Bloemencorso) | April 18 |
| Gerbera Show | April 23-28 |
| Fien and Teun (children's event) | April 27 - May 1 |

## Activities

- **Windmill** - traditional Dutch windmill on the grounds
- **Whisper boat** - quiet electric boat tours through surrounding waterways
- **Bicycle rental** - explore the bulb fields around Keukenhof
- **Guided tours** - organized walking tours of the gardens

## Family / Kids

- **Playground**
- **Animal pasture** - small petting area
- **Treasure hunt** - interactive activity for children

## Visitor Services

- **Wheelchairs** available
- **WiFi** available on site
- **Lockers** for bag storage

## Key Takeaways

- The 2026 season (March 19 - May 10) is a narrow window -- trip dates must align
- April 16-21 is especially eventful with the Alstroemeria & Calla Show
- The Flower Parade on April 18 is a major annual event worth planning around
- Visiting on a weekday (Mon-Wed) before 10:30 AM or after 4 PM minimizes crowds
- Early-season visitors see different flowers than late-season visitors -- neither is "wrong"
- Whisper boat and bicycle rental extend the visit beyond the gardens themselves
- Wheelchair accessibility and lockers make it practical for various visitor needs
