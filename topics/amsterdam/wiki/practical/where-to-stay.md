# Where to Stay in Amsterdam

## Overview

Amsterdam has extensive accommodation options from luxury hotels to hostels and houseboats. Due to high demand, especially during peak season (April--October) and school holidays, booking well in advance is strongly recommended. **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**

## Recommended Areas

| Area | Best For | Near |
|---|---|---|
| **Dam Square / Centrum** | First-time visitors, sightseeing | Royal Palace, Nieuwe Kerk, shops |
| **Museumplein** | Art lovers | Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh, Vondelpark |
| **Jordaan** | Atmosphere, local feel | Canals, Anne Frank House |
| **De Pijp** | Foodies, nightlife | Albert Cuyp Market |
| **Amsterdam-Oost** | Budget, local vibe | Oosterpark, Tropenmuseum |

## Featured Hotel: Die Port van Cleve

[Die Port van Cleve](../hotels/die-port-van-cleve.md) is a historic hotel ideally located behind the Nieuwe Kerk on [Dam Square](../places/dam-square.md), next to Magna Plaza.

- **History**: Founded 1864, connected to Gerard Heineken's brewery
- **Rooms**: 122 rooms themed around 8 historical periods
- **Dining**: Hulscher's Restaurant (steaks), Bouillon d'Amsterdam, Bar-Bodega De Blauwe Parade
- **Check-in**: 15:00 / **Check-out**: 11:00
- **Current offers**: Spring sale code "Spring30" for 30% off; 20% off Nieuwe Kerk tickets

**[Source: port-van-cleve]**

## Booking Tips

- Use comparison sites to find the best deals -- prices vary significantly across platforms **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
- The [I Amsterdam Card](budget-tips.md) does NOT include accommodation but can save money on transport and attractions
- Third-party booking sites may not display all taxes and fees -- check total price carefully **[Source: port-van-cleve]**
- Budget further from center: same category hotels are cheaper outside the canal ring **[Source: amszterdam-nevezetessegek]**
