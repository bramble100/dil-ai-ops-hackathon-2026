# Best Time to Visit Amsterdam & the Netherlands

## Season Overview

| Season | Months | Weather | Crowds | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Spring** | Apr--May | 10-17°C, mixed | High (tulip season) | Tulips, Keukenhof, parks |
| **Summer** | Jun--Aug | 17-22°C, warm | Highest | Outdoor cafes, festivals, cycling |
| **Autumn** | Sep--Oct | 10-17°C, rainy | Moderate | Museums, fewer tourists |
| **Winter** | Nov--Mar | 2-7°C, cold/wet | Low | Budget travel, ice skating |

## The Verdict

**April to October** is the recommended travel window, with the best weather and longest daylight hours. **[Source: viajeros-callejeros]**

If you can choose, **spring (April--May)** is ideal:
- Tulip fields in full bloom
- [Keukenhof](../places/keukenhof.md) open (2026: March 19 -- May 10) **[Source: keukenhof]**
- Pleasant temperatures without summer crowds
- King's Day (April 27) celebrations

## Keukenhof Timing

The flower garden follows nature's rhythms: **[Source: keukenhof]**

- **Early season (late March--mid April)**: Crocuses, daffodils, hyacinths, early tulips
- **Peak season (mid April--early May)**: Large tulip varieties in full bloom
- **Best time of day**: Before 10:30 AM or after 4:00 PM (fewer crowds, better light for photos)
- **Quietest days**: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday

## What to Pack

- Rain jacket or umbrella (rain is possible year-round)
- Layers (weather changes quickly)
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Flat shoes for cycling
