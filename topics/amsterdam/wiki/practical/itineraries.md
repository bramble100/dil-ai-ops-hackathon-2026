# Suggested Itineraries

## 1 Day in Amsterdam

Focus on the essentials: **[Multi-source]**

**Morning**
- Start at [Dam Square](../places/dam-square.md) -- see the Royal Palace, Nieuwe Kerk, National Monument
- Walk through the [canal ring](../places/canal-ring.md) to [Anne Frank House](../places/anne-frank-house.md) (book tickets far in advance!)

**Afternoon**
- [Rijksmuseum](../places/rijksmuseum.md) or [Van Gogh Museum](../places/van-gogh-museum.md) (pick one if time is short)
- Stroll through [Vondelpark](../places/vondelpark.md)

**Evening**
- [Canal cruise](../themes/canal-cruises.md) at sunset
- Dinner in the Jordaan or De Pijp neighborhoods

## 3 Days in Amsterdam

**Day 1: City Center**
- [Dam Square](../places/dam-square.md), Royal Palace (if open), Nieuwe Kerk
- [Begijnhof](../places/begijnhof.md)
- [Bloemenmarkt](../places/bloemenmarkt.md) (floating flower market)
- [Canal cruise](../themes/canal-cruises.md)
- Evening: [Red Light District](../places/red-light-district.md) walk, brown cafes

**Day 2: Museums**
- [Rijksmuseum](../places/rijksmuseum.md) (arrive at opening, 9:00)
- [Van Gogh Museum](../places/van-gogh-museum.md)
- Lunch at [Albert Cuyp Market](../places/albert-cuyp-market.md)
- [Anne Frank House](../places/anne-frank-house.md) (afternoon/evening slot)
- [Vondelpark](../places/vondelpark.md)

**Day 3: Day Trip**
- [Zaanse Schans](../places/zaanse-schans.md) (morning -- windmills, cheese, clogs)
- [Volendam & Marken](../places/volendam-marken.md) (afternoon -- fishing villages, ferry)
- Or: [Keukenhof](../places/keukenhof.md) if visiting in spring (full day recommended)

## 5 Days in Amsterdam & Netherlands

**Days 1-3**: As above

**Day 4: Southern Loop**
- Train to [Delft](../places/delft.md) (morning -- blue ceramics, old town)
- Continue to [Rotterdam](../places/rotterdam.md) (afternoon -- Cube Houses, Markthal)
- Or: organized tour combining Delft + Rotterdam + [The Hague](../places/hague.md)

**Day 5: Flex Day**
- [Keukenhof](../places/keukenhof.md) if spring and not yet visited
- [Utrecht](../places/utrecht.md) (Dom Tower, beautiful canals) -- 27 min by train
- [Cruquius Museum](../places/cruquius-museum.md) for engineering enthusiasts
- [NEMO Science Museum](../places/nemo-science-museum.md) if traveling with children
- Catch up on museums: Rembrandt House, Maritime Museum, Heineken Experience

## Tips for Planning

- **Book ahead**: Anne Frank House, Van Gogh Museum, Rijksmuseum all require advance online tickets **[Multi-source]**
- **Get a pass**: I Amsterdam Card (3 days / EUR 115) pays for itself quickly -- see [Budget Tips](budget-tips.md) **[Source: amszterdam-latnivalok]**
- **Spring priority**: If visiting April--May, make [Keukenhof](../places/keukenhof.md) a day 1 priority -- flower displays change weekly **[Source: keukenhof]**
- **Guided tours available**: Spanish-language guided tours cover many popular routes **[Source: viajeros-callejeros]**
