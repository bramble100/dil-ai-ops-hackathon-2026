## Overview

Alleghany Corporation is an insurance holding company acquired by Berkshire Hathaway in 2022. The acquisition was captained by **Joe Brandon**, whom Buffett had worked with for years prior. The deal added approximately **$17B to Berkshire's float**, pushing total float from $147B to $164B.

## Key Facts

- **Acquired:** 2022
- **Type:** Property-casualty insurance holding company
- **CEO at acquisition:** Joe Brandon
- **Float contribution:** ~$17B (pushed Berkshire total from $147B to $164B)
- **Strategic rationale:** Float expansion; Berkshire relationship with Brandon

## Significance

Alleghany represents Berkshire's continued strategy of growing float through acquisitions of well-run insurance operations. The deal extended Berkshire's **8,000-fold float increase** since purchasing National Indemnity in 1967 for $8.6M.

The acquisition also demonstrates Buffett's relationship-based deal sourcing — Joe Brandon was a known quantity, not a cold acquisition target.
