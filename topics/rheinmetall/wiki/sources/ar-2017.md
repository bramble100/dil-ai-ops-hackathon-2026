# Annual Report 2017 (Geschaeftsbericht 2017)

**File:** `raw/DE0007030009-JA-2017-EQ-D-01.pdf` (226 pages, German)

## Key Facts

- Revenue: 5,896 MioEUR (+5.2%)
- Operating Result: 400 MioEUR; Margin: 6.8%
- Net Cash: EUR 230 MioEUR
- Defence revenue: 3,036 MioEUR (margin 5.7%)
- Automotive revenue: 2,861 MioEUR (margin 8.7%)
- EUR 500m in e-mobility order nominations
- Strategy: Two-pillar (Automotive + Defence)

## Significance

Earliest report in the corpus. Captures Rheinmetall before the transformation -- a mid-cap dual-segment conglomerate with automotive as a significant contributor. Defence margin at 5.7% was still depressed by legacy contracts. Historical data extends to 2011, providing the longest time baseline.
