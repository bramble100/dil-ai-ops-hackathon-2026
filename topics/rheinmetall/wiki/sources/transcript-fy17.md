# Earnings Call Transcript - FY2017 Results

**File:** `raw/2018-03-15_Rheinmetall_Transcript_Conference_Call_FY17.pdf` (22 pages, English)
**Date:** 15 March 2018, 13:00-14:04 CET
**Speakers:** Armin Papperger (CEO), Helmut Merch (CFO)

## Key Takeaways

- "A very successful year for Rheinmetall" -- revenue +5.2% to EUR 5.9bn
- Cash flow surged >70% to EUR 276m (CCR from 46% to 69%)
- **Australia Land 400 Phase 2:** "A new milestone in our company's history"
- Net cash position: EUR 230m; Moody's outlook upgraded to positive (Ba1)
- European defence awakening cited: "first time European ministers said Europe must be stronger"
- **M&A signal:** "We will need it for the European consolidation"
- 2018 guidance: Defence growth 12-14%, margin 6-6.5%
- 10 analysts asked questions; key concerns: Land 400 risk, M&A risk, cash deployment

## Notable Quotes

> "We will need it for the European consolidation for sure and something will happen." -- Papperger

> "We only do such a deal if it makes sense for us... we are not under pressure." -- Papperger
