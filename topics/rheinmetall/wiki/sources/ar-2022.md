# Annual Report 2022 (Geschaeftsbericht 2022)

**File:** `raw/RheinmetallAGAnnualReport2022.pdf` (286 pages, German)

## Key Facts

- **Reporting period:** FY2022 -- the Zeitenwende year
- Revenue: 6,410 MioEUR (+13.3% YoY)
- Operating Result: 754 MioEUR (margin 11.8%)
- Employees: 25,486 (21,788 continuing)
- Stock price 31 Dec: EUR 186.05 (from EUR 83.06 -- +124%)
- FCF: -175 MioEUR (inventory build-up for capacity expansion)
- First year of modern order intake reporting: 5,629 MioEUR

## Notable Data

Provides the longest backward-looking data tables in any report: full financial time series back to 2016, including employee counts, cash flow, and balance sheet data. Critical reference document for pre-Ukraine baseline.

The negative FCF despite strong earnings reflects the initial working capital build-up as Rheinmetall began ramping production in response to the post-Ukraine order surge.
