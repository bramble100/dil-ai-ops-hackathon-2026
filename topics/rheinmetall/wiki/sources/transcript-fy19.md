# Earnings Call Transcript - FY2019 Results

**File:** `raw/2020-03-19_Rheinmetall_Transcript_Conference_Call_Q4.pdf` (25 pages, English)
**Date:** 18 March 2020, 14:00-14:55 CET
**Speakers:** Armin Papperger (CEO), Helmut Merch (CFO)

## Context

This call was held at the very onset of COVID-19 lockdowns -- a remarkable dual-tone document presenting strong FY2019 results alongside deep uncertainty about the pandemic.

## Key Takeaways

- "An excellent set of figures" -- revenue 6,255m (record), defence backlog crossed EUR 10bn
- Defence margin: 9.8% (record); Automotive margin: 6.7% (declining sharply)
- **Guidance withdrawn** due to COVID: "We cannot guide for the Corona impact"
- COVID crisis management: isolated production groups, 50/50 home office, China at ~70% productivity
- **Nitrocellulose stockpile revealed:** One year's supply held as risk management
- Export restrictions cost ~EUR 90m in 2019 (Saudi Arabia, South Africa)
- EUR 10bn+ pipeline for infantry fighting vehicle tenders globally
- Defence budgets resilient in crises: "If you have a crisis usually defense is more important"

## Notable Quotes

> "If you have a crisis usually defense is more important than if you have no crisis." -- Papperger

> "We are seen as a full auto company." -- Papperger on market perception

> "I have one year nitrocellulose on stock." -- Papperger on ammunition risk management
