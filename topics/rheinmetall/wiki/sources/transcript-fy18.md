# Earnings Call Transcript - FY2018 Results

**File:** `raw/2019-03-13_Rheinmetall_Transcript_Conference_Call_FY18.pdf` (18 pages, English)
**Date:** 13 March 2019, 14:00-14:54 CET
**Speakers:** Armin Papperger (CEO), Helmut Merch (CFO)

## Key Takeaways

- "An extraordinary year" -- revenue EUR 6.1bn, operating result EUR 492m, EPS +36%
- **Defence "super cycle" declared:** "2018 marked the beginning of this cycle"
- Record defence order intake: EUR 5.5bn (Land 400 + Australian trucks = ~EUR 2.6bn)
- Investment grade rating restored (April 2018)
- Automotive margin: 8.9% (record) despite headwinds
- Defence midterm margin raised to 8-9% (+200bps)
- Legacy contracts (Puma, Boxer) earned only "mid-single-digit" margins vs normal 15-20%
- KNDS European champion discussions "in full power" between German and French governments
- Somerset West tragedy acknowledged: 8 employees killed

## Notable Quotes

> "We spoke of the beginning of a super cycle in the Defence business and 2018 marked the beginning of this cycle." -- Papperger

> "In big programs you always have risks and we are very careful, we have to deliver." -- Papperger
