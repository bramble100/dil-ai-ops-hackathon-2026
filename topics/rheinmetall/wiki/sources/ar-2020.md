# Annual Report 2020 (Geschaeftsbericht 2020)

**File:** `raw/DE0007030009-JA-2020-PN-EQ-D-00.pdf` (264 pages, German)

## Key Facts

- Revenue: 5,405 MioEUR (continuing, restated) / 5,875 MioEUR (full group as originally reported)
- Operating Result: 446 MioEUR (continuing)
- Net Income: 1 MioEUR (after -283 MioEUR discontinued operations loss)
- EBIT: 398 MioEUR (continuing) / 89 MioEUR (full group incl. 337 MioEUR impairments)
- FCF: 217 MioEUR (continuing)

## Significance

The COVID year. The near-zero net income (1 MioEUR) despite reasonable defense performance crystallized the case for the automotive divestiture -- automotive losses consumed the entire defense profit. This report marks the beginning of the end for the dual-segment structure.
