# Annual Report 2021 (Geschaeftsbericht 2021)

**File:** `raw/RheinmetallAGAnnualReport2021.pdf` (261 pages, German)

## Key Facts

- Revenue: 5,658 MioEUR; Op. Result: 594 MioEUR; Margin: 10.5%
- FCF: 419 MioEUR (strong recovery from pandemic)
- ROCE: 19.5%
- "ONE Rheinmetall" restructuring implemented (Feb 2021)
- New 5-division structure; pistons classified as discontinued (May 2021)
- CEO Papperger reappointed for 5 years
- Dividend: EUR 3.30 (up from EUR 2.00)

## Significance

The last pre-Ukraine full-year report. Serves as the baseline for all before/after Ukraine comparisons. The ONE Rheinmetall restructuring was already underway, demonstrating that the strategic pivot predated the invasion -- though the invasion massively accelerated it.
